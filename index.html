Why Food Photography?

People eat with their eyes

Customers experience first through sight, captivate their senses with irresistible images that flaunt the essence of your cuisine.

More than just photography

I utilize a combination of food styling techniques, props, lighting & hours of post processing to present your dishes at their appetizing best.

Fire up your sales

Tantalizing potential diners with images of your dishes along with the ambience of your restaurant will increase walk-ins & drive revenue.

Hi, I'm Charith Heenpalla

I started my career in social media, but was so captivated by the photographic process that i took a leap of faith & traded a desk-job for a camera.

Today I capture culinary stories within a frame for a variety of f&b clients while blending two of my greatest passions; food & photography.
